Our Story

More Than a Restaurant,
A Place to Remember

Food excites individuals and brings happiness. Good food with quality get-together enhances contentment, increases bonding among friends, boosts family happiness, and more importantly initiates healthy discussions.

We at The Prince are here not only to satisfy your hunger, but more importantly to create memories — and add joy to your beautiful day. From dum biryani to Bengali fishes, Chinese cuisine to mouth-watering desserts, laajawab tandooris to authentic mocktails.

We are committed to preparing healthy dishes using quality ingredients without compromising taste. We do not use any kind of additives or food colours and are dedicated to preparing nutritious yet delicious dishes.
